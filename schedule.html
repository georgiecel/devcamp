🔥 Keynote: The Modern Development Burnout Machine

Sha Kong-Brooks

Our tools are better than ever, but often the work feels harder. I want to talk about the daily cognitive strain of modern development, from the constant context switches to expectations that leave no room for deep work. Perhaps along the way we can find ideas for designing workflows and teams that protect our focus, use AI wisely, and treat human attention as our most critical infrastructure.

👩‍💻 I Didn’t Hack You - I Just Used Your App as Designed

Samantha Czech

In this talk, I will demonstrate how a subtle authorisation design flaw that sails past SAST, SCA, and DAST becomes the entry point for chaining multiple real vulnerabilities and exposing other users’ data. It mirrors real data breaches and shows how attackers win by abusing trust and design mistakes, not by doing anything that looks like hacking.
